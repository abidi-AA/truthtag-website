How it works

Three layers.
One clear answer.

Every image passes through three independent detection systems in sequence. The most confident signal wins.

01

EXIF metadata forensics

Real camera photos carry device metadata — make, model, GPS coordinates, shutter speed. AI-generated images have none of this. Free, instant, zero API cost.

02

AI model detection

91% accurate visual analysis covering all major generators — Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, Flux. Updated continuously as new generators emerge.

03

Confidence threshold

Every result includes a score 0–100%. Your platform sets the action — flag at 60%, auto-reject at 90%, route to human review in between.
